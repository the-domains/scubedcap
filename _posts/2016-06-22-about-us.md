# About Us
![](https://the-grid-user-content.s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/7478b9a7-1901-49b3-aa1a-dbda212d195d.jpg)

S-Cubed Capital is a diversified investment holding company and family office located in Menlo Park, CA.
